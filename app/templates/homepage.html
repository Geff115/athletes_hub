Our mission is to help you connect with scouts from around the world, irrespective of your social status, background, or beliefs. We aim to breach the limitations that comes with playing your sport at a professional level here in Africa. Due to the large number of youths playing either Football or Basketball in Africa, Athletes Hub was created for this purpose, focusing strictly on Basketball and Football athletes.
